About The Author

Welcome to My World of Code

KK Deenadhayalan is an internationally recognized developer and educator whose technical writings have helped thousands of programmers across 15+ countries. Born in India and educated at prestigious tech institutes, he brings a unique perspective to software engineering education.

His debut coding notes, "JavaScript Essentials," became required reading at multiple coding bootcamps in 2020 and established him as a clear voice in technical education. Since then, he has published six comprehensive guides and two interactive coding workshops.

When not writing code, KK teaches advanced JavaScript at developer conferences and leads workshops around the world. He lives in Chennai with his laptop and a collection of mechanical keyboards.
